### **Project Title**: Walmart Sales Dashboard  

#### **Objective**:  
To analyze Walmart's sales data and create an interactive dashboard using Power BI to uncover key insights and trends for decision-making.

---

### **Key Activities**:  
1. **Data Preparation**:  
   - Loaded and cleaned the dataset, ensuring data quality and accuracy.  

2. **Visualizations**:  
   - Created interactive charts, including:  
     - **Monthly Sales Trends**: Highlighted seasonal patterns in sales.  
     - **Top 10 Best-Selling Products**: Identified the most profitable items.  
     - **Region-Wise Sales Distribution**: Analyzed performance across regions.  

3. **KPI Implementation**:  
   - Designed KPIs to track total sales, profit, and average discount rates.  

4. **Interactivity**:  
   - Enabled slicers for dynamic filtering by region, product category, and time.  

---

### **Key Findings 🔍**:  
- **💻 Most Sold Category**: Technology, leading the way in revenue generation.  
- **📈 Year-over-Year Growth**: Revealed consistent performance improvements.  
- **🛒 Monthly Trend**: December consistently recorded the highest sales each year.  
- **📅 2014 Performance**: Recorded the highest total sales of $250.63k and total profits of $43.90k, surpassing all previous years.  

---

### **Conclusion**:  
The dashboard provides actionable insights into Walmart's sales performance, helping stakeholders identify top-performing products, seasonal trends, and areas for growth to optimize their sales strategies.
